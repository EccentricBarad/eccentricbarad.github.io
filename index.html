Our Stake Pool

We are two passionate software developers who are excited about the cryptocurrency space.

Valence is our effort to help decentralize the Cardano network further and raise awareness about the prospects this blockchain provides.

VALENCE stake pool infrastructure is set up with multiple relay and core nodes distributed across the globe utilizing the reliability of Google Cloud and the dedication of our team to keep our machines running. Each server runs on 5GB RAM, 2vCPUs, 5TB of transfer with 1Gbps bandwidth (around 125MB/s), and 50GB SSD storage. The cloud-based infrastructure allows easy scaling of the system if need be in the future.
