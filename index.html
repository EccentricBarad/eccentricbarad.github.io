The technology

Valence stake pool infrastructure is set up with multiple relay and core nodes distributed across the globe utilizing the reliability of Google Cloud Platform and the dedication of our team to keep our machines running. Each server runs on 5GB RAM, 2vCPUs, 5TB of transfer with 1Gbps bandwidth (around 125MB/s), and 50GB SSD storage. The cloud-based infrastructure allows easy scaling of the system if need be in the future.

What is ADA delegation?

Delegation is the process by which ADA holders delegate the stake associated with their ada to a stake pool. It allows ada holders that do not have the skills or desire to run a node to participate in the network and be rewarded in proportion to the amount of stake delegated.

Is my ADA at risk?

Your ADA never needs to leave your wallet. Be cautios of any pools/sites that ask you to transfer your ADA.

Pool Size

It is a misconception that small pools do not provide as good of a reward as established and well saturated stake pools. In fact, small stake pools that produce a block generated a higher per-delegator reward.
